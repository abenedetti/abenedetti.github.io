uCount Visualizer & uGeoTagger

uTest1 has been an inspiration for my first two works, two shiny apps about uTesters' stats and locations (yep in uTest there are no users... 😉 but uTesters !). I made them during my spare time and it was real fun, nonetheless they were a nice application of some of the new skills I studied.

1 uTest is a professional network for Testers, born on 2007. I signed up on 2015, and during the first two years, I became further and further engaged in the community as well as in the testing activities with over 300 test cycles made. I also was able to reach the silver rating in the functional area. From late 2017 I've reduced the testing activities as a started sharpening my skills in the analytics field, but I kept being active in the community. Should you be interested in uTest take at look at my utest profile and my other articles.
